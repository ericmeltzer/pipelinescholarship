FAQ

What should my personal statement contain?
Answer：We want to gain a better understanding of you: who you are, how your family background has influenced your studies, whether you've had obstacles to your continued studies and if so, how you've overcome them, your short and long term plans for study, etc.

Who should I ask for a letter of recommendation from?
Answer：Ideally a teacher that has a good understanding of your ability as a student, but conceivably also any mentor in your life other than your relatives or parents!

How much money does the scholarship provide？
Answer：The scholarship provides one year's of tuition, capped at 7000 RMB.
